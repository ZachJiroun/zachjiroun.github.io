SubtleGlass is a Google Glass hack exploring new possibilites for improving communication for hearing-impaired individuals.

Through the integrated microphone, SubtleGlass converts nearby audio to text and then displays it. Other applications of this hack include displaying subtitles during movies, transcribing conversations, and assisting students in taking notes.
